Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality can take 6-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your web development services?

+

Our services include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, content management system setup, testing across devices and browsers, training, and post-launch support. We provide a comprehensive solution from concept to launch.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

+

Yes! We offer various maintenance packages including security updates, content updates, performance monitoring, and technical support. We believe in long-term partnerships with our clients.

Can you help with SEO and digital marketing?

+

Absolutely! We build SEO-friendly websites from the ground up and offer ongoing SEO services, content strategy, and digital marketing consultation to help you achieve your business goals.

What if I need changes after the website is live?

+

We provide training on how to manage your website, and we're always available for updates and changes. Minor content updates are often included in our maintenance packages, while larger changes are handled as separate projects.

Do you work with clients outside your local area?

+

Yes! We work with clients worldwide. Our process is designed to work seamlessly remotely, with regular video calls, project management tools, and clear communication throughout the project.
